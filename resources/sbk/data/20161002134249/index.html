How to record and download Netflix, Hulu, or another streaming video website:

  1. Download PlayOn and install it on your PC. You’ll be recording in minutes.
  2. Open PlayOn, click on the Channels Tab, click on Edit Channel Settings in the upper right hand corner, then enter your Netflix Account info — this lets PlayOn access and record your Netflix account – your credentials are kept secure.
  3. Browse to Netflix, Hulu (or any other streaming channel) for the show or movie you’d like to record.
  4. Click Record. Now – you can close the PlayOn window and use your PC to do whatever you like. PlayOn will record the entire video in the background and you’ll get a notification when it’s complete.
  5. Once the recording is complete, you will have an .mp4 file that you can download to any device you'd like. Or cast recordings to your TV with a compatible device.
