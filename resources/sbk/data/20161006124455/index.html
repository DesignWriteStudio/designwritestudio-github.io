Abstract:
The article discusses the workshop "Does ethnography have to be linear? Experimenting with hypertext ethnography." Topics discussed include hypertext ethnographhy as a format for academic works, nonlinear narratives to the discipline of anthropology, and scarcity of venues for decoding academic work in non-conventional formats.
