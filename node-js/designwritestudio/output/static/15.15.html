In creating an arrangement capable of working in depth and not only on the surface of the thread of discourse, the author of a tabular hypertext must take the utmost care in establishing the different layers and distributing the information between the base level and the other layers. These choices will vary with the type of text and target audience. The levels of information may be distributed on the axis of concrete/ abstract or divided between narrative and documents or between scholarly text, experimental data, and reference works, or between didactic text, examples, and exercises, and so on.
